Mathematics

My research interests have to do with non-linear analysis and computer assisted proof. For instance, I am interested in invariant manifolds of dynamical systems. My dissertation dealt with invariant manifolds of the area-preserving Henon map and the Standard map. As well as a computation scheme to compute Siegel Disks in simple quadratic maps.
